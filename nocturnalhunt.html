Nocturnal Hunt

Game AI Programmer | Gameplay / Systems Programmer

Nocturnal Hunt is a first person stealth game where you become the wolf in a forest full of deadly hunters. Find and save your cub!

The Games Academy awarded Nocturnal Hunt with the title "Excellence Project" together with a budget of EUR 1000 for an extended development time.
