Spencer is a 3D third person brawler. The player takes control of a teddy bear to fight against evil shadows and defend the sleeping child. He fights through the child's room and enters a dangerous dreamworld.

At the "Deutschen Entwicklerpreis 2015" in cologne we won the second place in the category "Newcomer" sponsored by "Ubisoft Blue Byte".
